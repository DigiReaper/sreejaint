Academics

Sreeja International School provides a comprehensive academic program for students from Nursery to 10th class. The school follows a curriculum that is designed to provide a strong foundation in core subjects such as mathematics, science, social studies, and languages. The academic program is tailored to meet the needs and learning styles of each student, with a focus on developing critical thinking skills, problem-solving abilities, and creativity.

IIT-JEE Foundation

Sreeja International School provides IIT-JEE Foundation coaching for students who aspire to pursue engineering as a career. The school's innovative methodology and experienced faculty members help students develop a strong foundation in core subjects such as mathematics, physics, and chemistry. The program is designed to provide comprehensive training for the highly competitive IIT-JEE exam.

Undergraduate Education

NEET Foundation

Sreeja International School also provides NEET Foundation coaching for students who aspire to pursue medical as a career. The school's experienced faculty members provide comprehensive training in core subjects such as biology, physics, and chemistry, which are essential for success in the highly competitive NEET exam. The program is designed to help students develop a strong foundation in these subjects, which will help them in their future medical studies.

Graduate Education

Sports Coaching

Sreeja International School places a strong emphasis on sports and provides coaching in a wide range of sports, including cricket, football, basketball, badminton, and table tennis. The school's experienced coaches use a variety of training methodologies to help students develop their physical fitness, coordination, teamwork, and leadership skills. The school also provides opportunities for students to participate in inter-school and state-level sports competitions, which helps them gain exposure and build their confidence.

Campus Information

Sreeja International School is located in Khammam and boasts a modern and well-equipped campus. The campus features spacious classrooms, science, computer and language labs, a library, a cafeteria, and a playground. The school provides transportation facilities for students and ensures their safety with trained drivers and staff. The campus is designed to provide a comfortable and safe learning environment that fosters students' intellectual, emotional, and physical development.
