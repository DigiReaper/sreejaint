Sreeja International School

Welcome to Sreeja International School! We are a leading educational institution located in Khammam, Telangana, committed to providing world-class education to students from Nursery to 10th class. Here are some common questions people may have about us:

What is our mission?

Our mission is to prepare our students for success in the 21st century. We believe in holistic learning, which means providing students with a well-rounded education that includes academic excellence, co-curricular activities, and social and emotional development. We strive to instill in our students a love of learning, a sense of curiosity, and a commitment to lifelong learning.

What is our academic program like?

Our academic program is designed to provide a strong foundation in core subjects such as mathematics, science, social studies, and languages. We use a variety of teaching methodologies to ensure that students are engaged and motivated to learn. Our program also includes specialized coaching for competitive exams such as IIT-JEE and NEET.

What co-curricular activities do we offer?

We offer a wide range of co-curricular activities, including music, dance, drama, and art. These activities help students develop their leadership, teamwork, and social skills. We also provide sports coaching in a variety of sports, such as cricket, football, basketball, badminton, and table tennis.

Who are our faculty members?

Our faculty members are highly qualified and experienced, with a passion for teaching and a commitment to student success. They use a variety of teaching methodologies to ensure that students are engaged and motivated to learn. Our faculty members are also dedicated to providing individualized attention and support to each student.

What is our campus like?

Our campus is modern and well-equipped, with spacious classrooms, science, computer, and language labs, a library, a cafeteria, and a playground. We provide transportation facilities for students, with a fleet of well-maintained buses that cover different parts of the city. We ensure that all transportation is safe and secure, with trained drivers and staff accompanying the students.

We hope this gives you a good idea of who we are and what we stand for at Sreeja International School. Please feel free to contact us if you have any further questions or would like to schedule a visit.
