About Us

At Sreeja International School, we believe in holistic learning and provide a wide range of co-curricular activities, including music, dance, drama, and art, to help students develop their leadership, teamwork, and social skills. Our campus is modern and well-equipped, with all necessary facilities to provide a comfortable and safe learning environment for our students.Our mission is to prepare our students for success in the 21st century.
